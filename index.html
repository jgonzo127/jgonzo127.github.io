A Little Bit About Myself:


I'm a Front End web designer interested in learning as much as I can about the art of coding. The idea of combining design and content on something as powerful and frequently used as the web is fascinating to me.

Working in web design is an idea that dawned on me very recently. I didn't realize how much myself and everyone around me relied on the internet for so many things. Of course I need to make stuff on the web!

Friendship, stability, and hard work are all very important to me. When I'm not coding, I'm usually listening to music, watching movies, keeping up with local sports teams, or playing video games!
